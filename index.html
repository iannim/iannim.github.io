About me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, Modeling, Electronic and System Engineering (DIMES) of the University of Calabria, Italy.

I received the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Technologies from the University of Calabria, Italy, in 2018. During my Ph.D. I was a visiting researcher in SecLab, University of California, Santa Barbara.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Calabria, Italy and at the University of Verona, Italy. In 2023, I was a visiting professor at the Instituto Superior Técnico of the University of Lisbon, Portugal.

My main research interests include Binary Analysis and Exploitation, Obfuscation, Watermarking, Malware, Trusted Execution Environments and IoT Security.
